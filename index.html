What happens after the 7-day trial? ▼

You'll be charged the plan price automatically. You can cancel anytime before the trial ends and you won't be charged a cent. No hoops, no phone calls — just cancel from your billing portal.

Can I switch plans later? ▼

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime from the billing portal. If you upgrade mid-cycle, you'll be prorated. If you downgrade, the change takes effect at your next billing date.

What markets does the scanner cover? ▼

The scanner covers 200+ ASX-listed stocks across all sectors. It runs proven mechanical strategies — CRT reversals, breakouts, trend continuations, and momentum plays — and detects cross-asset divergences between correlated markets.

How do the AI predictions work? ▼

They combine technical indicators (RSI, MACD, EMA alignment), overnight futures flow, and macro context to generate a daily verdict for each ASX index. The AI shows its reasoning so you can judge for yourself.

Is my payment info secure? ▼

Payments are handled entirely by Stripe — we never see or store your card details. Stripe is PCI Level 1 certified, the highest level of security in the payments industry.

Can I cancel anytime? ▼

Yes. Cancel from the billing portal in two clicks. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees, no retention calls.
