What's in it

⚡

ASX Scanner

Scans 200+ stocks for CRT reversals, breakouts, and momentum plays. Spots cross-asset divergences between correlated markets to catch high-probability reversals early. Filter for only A-grade setups.

🌙

Overnight Futures

S&P, Nasdaq, Dow, Gold, Oil, Bitcoin, DXY — with sparklines. Updates every 3 minutes. Know if the world went risk-on or risk-off while you were asleep.

🧠

AI Predictions

Daily ASX 200 and sector index calls. Combines RSI, MACD, EMA alignment, overnight flow, and macro context. Shows its working so you can agree or disagree.

📰

News Feed

18 finance-only sources. Every headline classified by impact with a one-liner on how it moves markets. No royal family stories, no celebrity junk.

📅

Economic Calendar

Filter by impact and currency. USD high-impact only? Done. Shows forecast vs actual with beat/miss colouring. Highlights the next event so you don't get blindsided.

🏦

Sector & Flow

Where's the money rotating? Sector performance, fund screener, and institutional flow analysis. See what the big end of town is doing before you size up.
